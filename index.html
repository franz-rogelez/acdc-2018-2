Course goal

The purpose of this course is to involve students in all life cycle of the development of applications for convergent devices, the components that make them up and give them some analytical tools that allow them to obtain their own criteria in decision making in a software project. focused on mobile devices, with real needs and facing customer requirements with innovation, creativity and knowledge, thus satisfying functional requirements and quality attributes. Also understanding how these needs are transformed into user mobility and satisfy the client's requirement.
